Building Upon Success

Airport Residency is a joint venture between renowned real estate developers, Machiyara Group and Kohistan Builders. With extensive.
Experience spanning over four decades in Karachi’s real estate development industry, both the developers have well established
reputations in construction and sale of high quality residential and commercial projects. Building upon this unmatched legacy of
professionalism, trust and innovation, the JV is proud to launch its signature project – Airport Residency.

Premium Residence

Premium 2 and 3 Bedroom Apartments designed following International Standards by Acclaimed Architects, Engineers and Real Estate
Developer.

Peace In Integration

Airport Residency, located just off Shahrah-e-Faisal near Jinnah International Airport, offers a safe and serene environment with multiple facilities at short distances as a convenience for an urban and high-end lifestyle.
